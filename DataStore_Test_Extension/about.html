About DataStore

DataStore is an all in one application designed to give you the most control over your personal data and online privacy.

To use DataStore:

DataStore provides the option to collect personal data on yourself and gives you the power to do as you wish with it. Adjust your tracker settings and begin automatically collecting data. Collected data is stored locally with your google account, so it is inaccessible to any third parties including DataStore.

1. Navigate to Data Settings Page in Datastore Options Menu

2. Select which data points you would like to track while browsing and click 'Save'

3. Data will automatically begin collecting based on your preferences.


To Alter Stored Data:

1. Users may want to alter collected data if it contains sensitive or personal information.

2. To do so, expand the collected data view in the Data Settings page

3. Select a data point you wish to alter, you will be taken to an external view.

4. Change the contents of the collected data and hit 'Save'

5. To clear ALL stored data, click 'Clear Stored Data' in the Data Settings page.


To Restrict Third-Party Trackers:

1. Navigate to the 'Cookie Manager' section of the DataStore options

2. Filter or locate the cookies you wish to restrict

3. Select 'Block' on a domain to block an entire domain from tracking you

4. Expand a domain and select 'Block' to block individual cookies

5. Blocked cookies will automatically be kept from entering your browsing session

6. Blocking some cookies may alter the functionality of some pages, use caution when blocking


To Set Up Personal Demographics:

Providing demographic information about yourself is an easy way to add value to your data.

Add as little or as much as you wish, the only requirement is an email address so that you may be contacted by interested buyers. 

1. Navigate to the 'Demographics' page in the DataStore options

2. Fill out Email Address Section 

3. Fill out any other optional sections

4. Hit 'Save'

Your demographic information will persist across devices.


To List Your Collected Data for Sale:

Once you have collected a sufficient amount of data and provided an email address, your data can be offered for sale on the DataStore platform. 

1. Navigate to the DataStore options

2. Locate the 'Post Stored Data' button and click

3. Your collected data will automatically download and you will be redirected to the platform

4. Upload your recently downloaded data to the platform, verify your information, and submit

5. Your data is now available to buyers, you will be contacted by interested parties.

None of your collected data is stored on the platform, only metadata, and demographic information. The only person who can give away your stored data is you.
